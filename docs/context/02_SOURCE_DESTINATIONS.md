## The "Iceberg" Strategy

Most influencer scraping tools skim the top 10% of the web (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Trending). Your "Hunters" need to scrape the bottom 90%—the "Iceberg"—where the actual industry works.

- **Target:** Professionals who credit their crew, not just themselves.
- **Signal:** The presence of a "credits list" (e.g., "Gaffer: [Name]", "Colorist: [Name]") is the single strongest indicator of professional craft.
